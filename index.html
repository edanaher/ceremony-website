Kayak the Charles

This is a picture of us cave kayaking in Slovenia, but in September it is the perfect temperature to take a kayak out on the Charles. Kelly suggests going from the landing in Kendall Square, and then getting chicken and waffles from Commonwealth after you've burned off all those calories.

Hike the White Mountains

We enjoy hiking in the White Mountains in New Hampshire, two hours north of Boston. It is also the home of Bretton Woods, the site of the agreement that began modern monetary policy in the United States. The picture is of Evan hiking Mount Hale.
