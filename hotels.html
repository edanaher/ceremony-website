Hotel Blocks

Sorry these took so long, Boston is expensive to stay in this time of year, and we are sorry about this. If there are enough people staying at another hotel, we can also route the bus on Saturday there.

Expensive Hotel

Boston Park Plaza

Location: Back Bay, next to Arlington Station on the Green Line

At $279 w/o tax. Fees are waived. We have a block of ten here. Next to the Boston Garden, it very central to many of the sights in downtown Boston.

Cheaper Hotel

Holiday Day Inn Somerville

Location: Sullivan Square, near Sullivan Square Station on the Orange Line

At $190 a night w/o tax. We have a block of 20 here (link to come). This is right off the Orange Line, but also has a shuttle to North Station/MGH. If you are planning on renting a car (again please do not drive to the ceremony), they have free parking.
