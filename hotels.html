Hotel Blocks

We apologize that these took so long. Boston is expensive to stay in this time of year, and we're sorry about that. If you want to find other guests to split the cost of a room, you can also use the forum. Also, if there are enough people staying at another hotel, we will route the bus there on Saturday.

Expensive Hotel

Boston Park Plaza

Location: Back Bay, next to Arlington Station on the Green Line

At $279 w/o tax. Fees are waived. We have a block of ten here. Next to the Boston Common, it very central to many of the sights in downtown Boston.

Cheaper Hotel

Holiday Day Inn Somerville

Location: Sullivan Square, near Sullivan Square Station on the Orange Line

At $190 a night w/o tax. We have a block of 20 here. This is right off the Orange Line, but also has a shuttle to North Station/MGH. If you are planning on renting a car (again please do not drive to the ceremony), they have free parking.
