Location: Bradley Estate Canton, MA Time: As soon as the pictures are finished - 10 pm

Even if you are local we'd love it if you could park your car in Boston at one of the Hotels (we will pay for it), and take one of the buses. We want to make sure people have time to sober up before driving, as we will not be happy if anyone gets a DUI or in an accident due to drinking & driving
